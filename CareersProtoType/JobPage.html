Floral Designer

Save

Floral designers create arrangements for weddings, funerals, holidays, and everyday occasions. It's important for a floral designer to have a natural love of flowers. It also helps to be a people person. Flowers are usually given for a special occasion, so put your smile on and help design beautiful gifts.

What can you do in school?

Take art courses.

What can you do after school?

Start your own business. Work for a floral shop

What to expect

Deal with customers
Cut, trim and arrange flowers
Work under pressure and over holidays

Where can I practice this?

Contact your nearby floral shop. Always a good idea to get some experience while you are studying.
